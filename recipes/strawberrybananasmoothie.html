Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Description

This recipe is for a tasty and incredibly simple strawberry banana smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Cut your strawberries into halves.
  2. Cut your banana into slices.
  3. Blend ingredients together until your desired texture is achieved.
  4. Serve and...
  5. Enjoy!
