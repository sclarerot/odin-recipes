Ramen

Description

This recipe is for my very own homemade ramen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack ramen noodles (discard flavor packet)
  • Hot sauce
  • Lemon juice
  • Spices (garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, pepper, lemon pepper, ground cumin, seasoned salt)

Steps

  1. Boil roughly 1 1/2 cups water.
  2. Add your noodles.
  3. Add each spice, as well as hot sauce and lemon juice, to your liking, and stir consistently.
  4. Halfway through cooking, add more of every spice, with the exceptions of lemon pepper, chili powder, cumin, and seasoned salt. Optionally add more hot sauce.
  5. Once the water has almost entirely evaporated, your ramen is done.
  6. After placing your ramen in a bowl, add more garlic, onion powder, salt, pepper, and optionally hot sauce.
  7. Enjoy!
