Grilled Cheese

Description

This recipe is for a delicious at-home grilled cheese, with elements of both spice and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices brioche bread
  • 2 slices muenster cheese
  • Butter
  • Spices (garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, lemon pepper)
  • 4 jalapeno slices

Steps

  1. Lightly butter your bread, enough for the seasonings to stick. You DON'T want it to be greasy.
  2. Apply spices, to your liking, on the outside of both slices of bread.
  3. In between your bread, add both slices of muenster with your jalapenos nested in between the two slices of cheese.
  4. Cook in frying pan, flipping as needed, until cheese is melted. Ideally, your bread will still be somewhat soft at this point.
  5. Enjoy!
